Bába is the weaver of the deepest of shame, the embodiment of Arachne's and the eastern European Vasorrú Bába's daughter. She appears at night, crawling up on your chest, when you are in the mystic space between dreaming and being awake, she spins silk from the shame in your heart and throat. 
You shall not be afraid, she is working for you, alchemising feelings that eat away your ability to show up for yourself. Once you wake up, you will find yourself in a bewitched state, lustful for life, feeling light again.
